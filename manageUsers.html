Manage Users

Here you can view, edit, and add onto the information any of the users currently saved in the database. To edit a user, simply click on the piece of information you want to edit. To confirm your changes press enter or click off of the input, and to cancel your changes hit escape. Note that when adding a new user, after you confirm the username it will automatically get placed in the correct alphabetical position. Also note that passwords are blurred for privacy concerns, when you edit the password of a user you will be able to actually see it.
