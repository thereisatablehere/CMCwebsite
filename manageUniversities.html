Manage Universities

Here you can view, edit, and add onto the information any of the universitites currently saved in the database. To edit a university, simply click on the piece of information you want to edit. To confirm your changes press enter or click off of the input, and to cancel your changes hit escape. Note that when adding a new university, after you confirm the school name it will automatically get placed in the correct alphabetical position.
